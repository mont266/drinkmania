Never Have I Ever

Play the classic Never Have I Ever drinking game! Players all need to hold their hands up. Click the "Never Have I Ever" button to generate a 'Never Have I Ever'. Any players that have done it must put a finger down. The first player to lose all 5 fingers must finish their drink.

How To Play

This game is rather easy and simple!

Each player will take turns to click the Draw 2 Cards button. The game will pick 1 drink card and 1 dare card at random and the player will have to pick one to do. Once the player has fulfilled the card, the next player will then click the Draw 2 Cards button and so on...
